Our Products

HEXO+ is a flying camera that follows and films you autonomously. Just attach your GoPro to your drone, set your framing in our app and you're good to go! This package includes :

  • HEXO+ drone
  • 3D gimbal with GoPro Mount
  • HEXO+ App "The Director's Toolkit"

Caracteristics

Easy to Use

Set the framing on your smartphone, the drone does the rest, from take-off to landing.

Smart and Autonomous

No remote control necessary; HEXO+ follows your movements to maintain your framing.

Extra Stable Footage

Hexacopter design and 2D or 3D brushless gimbal for smooth filming.
